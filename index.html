Mason Yarnell

Good question, @Ryan. This was an iterative process and a good functionality spec from the client always comes in handy. The client had a basic prototype built. That helped me understand the big idea. One example of a major change was that they had the Filter by Type navigation hidden away in a small dropdown. During product testing, no one could find it. It was clear that had to be more prominently integrated into the dashboard. I designed it as a vertical navigation, similar to an email app. This felt familiar and it can be hidden to focus on the main content area if the user wishes.
